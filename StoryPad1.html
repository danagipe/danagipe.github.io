Then there is a loud screech and Nelson flies out of the jungle and grabs the alien and pulls it off Doctor Redrix and then shocks it! Nelson lets the alien go and it is dead. After a minute he stopped bleeding. He held my hand and told me “That was amazing Genn. You saved my life. Now we will have to live together on this planet until someone comes to rescue us.”
